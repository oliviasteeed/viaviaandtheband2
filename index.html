(the point)

This album (written between August 2023 and January 2024 and recorded from December 2023 to July 2024) is about reality, whereas most of the last one (It’s Never Enough) was about fantasy. It is the result of being pulled into somebody else's world, which is fun and exciting and overwhelming and scary and uncertain and sometimes doesn't work out. This how I dealt with that.

These songs are a snapshot of me learning to live with that uncertainty, vulnerability, and impermanence, and actually enjoy them. I want to feel something different, because it gives me something to explore with lyrics and music, my absolute favourite thing in the world. I've realized my life is nothing but cycles of experience and expression and I really have no choice in the matter.

I wanted Anxious Avoidant to have a more heavy sound, because we're all adults now. To me, AA feels like INE's big sister and I love that. Biggest thanks in the world to Elliot for absolutely slaughtering every chord strum, piano hit and drum beat. He does most of the tracks in one take, which is insane when you're playing at 180BPM, for example (like on Mindfuck!).

I’m glad this, our second album, is finally out because I’m already done writing the next one (it’s called sex, drugs, and open mindedness LMAO) and halfway through the next (tentatively called citygirl). I just have a lot to say apparently.

Until next time (hopefully soon),
xOlivia

(the band)

You know the set by now. I (Olivia) write the songs on guitar (usually on my pink Jazzmaster, Merona), and make a dionysian demo on Reaper that’s relatively in time. Then Elliot (the band) and I work together to remake the song, using his technical expertise to add more musical variety and flavour than my campfire chords can offer. And then we lose our minds listening to it, amazed that we made another banger.
